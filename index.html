Your Specialist Tanele Shabangu

Tanele Shabangu is a registered counsellor who is registered with (HPCSA). Tanele completed a Bachelor of Social Science Honours (BPsych Equivalent) from the South African College of Applied Psychology (SACAP). Tanele has over 8 years of experience in the counselling industry. She works with children from the age of 7-14, teenagers and adults. How getting councelling can help you..

Insight

Counseling can provide individuals with valuable insight into their thoughts, emotions, and behaviors.

Empowerment

It empowers individuals to take control of their lives, make informed decisions, and work towards personal growth.

Coping skills

It helps individuals develop effective coping strategies to deal with stress, anxiety, and other emotional difficulties.

Support

Counseling offers a supportive environment where individuals can share their concerns, fears, and challenges without judgment.

Communication

Counseling enhances communication skills, enabling individuals to express themselves more effectively and improve their relationships.

Self-awareness

It promotes self-reflection and self-awareness, facilitating personal understanding and promoting a deeper sense of self.
