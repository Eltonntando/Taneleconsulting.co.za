Meet Tanele Shabangu

Tanele Shabangu is a registered counsellor who is registered with (HPCSA). Tanele completed a Bachelor of Social Science Honours (BPsych Equivalent) from the South African College of Applied Psychology (SACAP). Tanele has over 8 years of experience in the counselling industry. She works with children from the age of 7-14, teenagers and adults. She has also been awarded First Aid Level One Certificate from the NeuroEducare- Family and Learning Centre.
A dedicated and highly qualified registered counselor with a passion for helping others, also proudly registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA). Tanele holds a Bachelor of Social Science Honors in Psychology [BPsych Equivalent] from the prestigious South African College of Applied Psychology (SACAP).
Tanele is not only committed to her clients' well-being but also to creating awareness about mental health on a larger scale. You can catch her on radio Ekasi FM 101.1 (Matsulu) every Tuesday from 13:10 pm, where she actively promotes mental health and provides valuable insights and tips.
In today's digital age, Tanele recognizes the importance of accessible counseling services, and thus, she also offers online counseling and therapy sessions via Zoom and Teams platforms. For those who prefer face-to-face interactions, she grants in-person counseling services upon request.
With over 8 years of experience in the counseling industry, Tanele has honed her skills in working with diverse age groups, including children aged 7-14, teenagers, and adults. Her ability to communicate effectively in up to 6 languages, including English, Siswati, Sizulu, Sixhosa, Sesotho, and Sepedi, further enhances her ability to connect with clients from various backgrounds.
Moreover, Tanele's commitment to personal and professional growth is evident through her additional achievements. She has earned the First Aid Level One Certificate from the esteemed NeuroEducare- Family and Learning Centre, further enhancing her ability to provide comprehensive care to her clients.
As a compassionate and skilled counselor, Tanele Shabangu is dedicated to guiding individuals on their journey towards mental and emotional well-being. Whether you choose to engage with her on the radio, through online platforms, or in person, Tanele's expertise and caring approach makes her an excellent choice for counseling services.
